🍎 My Work

3D Graphics Prototyping Engineer - Apple

(2015 to present)

As part of the Camera Incubation team, I design and develop interactive demos, simulations, visualization tools, and exploratory prototypes to evaluate next-generation camera hardware across a wide range of devices. Much of my work is confidential, but it lives at the intersection of 3D graphics, hardware validation, and immersive user experiences. While the prototypes are interactive and often visually engaging, they are grounded in rigorous scientific methodology. We validate our results against ground truth data to ensure accuracy. I use whatever language or software best fits the problem, often Unity, Blender, Python, or custom tools, and thrive as a generalist who bridges disciplines.
